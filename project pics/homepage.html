Bake ur TATTOO

If you think tattoos are more than just ink, then you have already got our attention. For us, tattoos are a part of an individual, a beautiful accessory & story engraved on body. At Inkinn, we aim to accentuate your love for tattoos by showing you the beauty it holds & teaching the mastery required. Our artists start by teaching you the basic drawing skill sharpening methods before moving onto ink & needles. If you have steady hands in drawing or if you are an artist already, then welcome to the club! But being a tattoo artist means you not only have to take care about keeping your hand steady, but also handling skin with care, using safe ink & needles, prescribing correct aftercare, what style would suit the customer etc. Keeping safety of artists & customers in mind, we use vegan & organic ink, sterilized needles & high quality machines. We uncover the tattoo techniques for cover-up tattoos, colored tattoos, calligraphy, portrait, etc. and train you to gain absolute proficiency.

our speciality


Step 1

Send us your tattoo idea

Simply head over to the “Start My Design” page and send us a brief description of your design idea. Whether it’s a completely detailed vision or just a basic overview of your idea; our team will make sure we understand your dream tattoo.

Step 2

Receive a quote

All quotes are no obligation to buy. Once we receive your tattoo idea our design team will look over the size and complexity of the tattoo. They will determine how much the design will cost and send you all the information you need to get started today.

Step 3

Work with a professional artist

Once you receive the quote from our design team; you simply make your deposit and you’re ready to get started. Paying the deposit triggers a message to our Art Director to review your design and select the best artist for you.

Step 4

Get your dream tattoo design

You will work one-on-one with your artist until your design is everything you dreamed it would be. Once your Custom Tattoo Design is complete and you love it, we will send you all your final designs in High Definition with a certificate of authenticity.
